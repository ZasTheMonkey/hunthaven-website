Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book private hunting land in Florida?

Browse available listings on LeaseWild, select your check-in and check-out dates, and submit a pre-reservation. Bookings officially open July 1, 2026. Your spot is held at no charge until then.

Can I rent private land for hog hunting in Florida?

Yes. LeaseWild has Florida properties that allow hog hunting, including night hog access. Filter listings by activity to find hog hunting land near you.

How much does it cost to book a hunting lease in Florida?

Prices vary by property, acreage, and season. Guests pay the listed nightly rate plus a 15% service fee and ~$18 trip protection. There are no monthly subscription fees.

Can I list my Florida hunting land on LeaseWild?

Yes — landowners can list immediately, no approval required. Create your landowner account, upload photos and details, and go live within minutes. You keep 85% of every booking.

Does LeaseWild offer private fishing water rentals in Florida?

Yes. Browse our fishing section for private bass ponds, freshwater lakes, and creek access across Florida. Guests can book exclusive water access by the day or weekend.

What is the cancellation policy?

LeaseWild uses a flexible cancellation policy. Cancel more than 48 hours before check-in for a full refund. Cancellations within 48 hours are non-refundable.
