Trip protection fee

A flat fee paid by the guest at checkout. Goes directly to our licensed insurance partner — not to HuntHaven. Issues a real $1M liability policy per trip, naming both the guest and landowner. Required on every booking.

Service fee

15% of the listing price, charged to the guest. Covers platform operations, support, state rules data, and digital waiver processing. You always see this before confirming.
