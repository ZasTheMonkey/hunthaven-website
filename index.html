FAQ

Common questions

How do I book private hunting land in Florida? +

Browse listings, select your dates, and complete checkout with Stripe. Bookings officially open July 1, 2026. Join the waitlist to be first in line when bookings open.

Do I need a hunting license on private land? +

Florida state regulations apply to private land for most species. Regulations are displayed on every listing. Fully private, non-navigable ponds do not require a Florida fishing license.

What is the cancellation policy? +

Flexible — full refund if you cancel 48+ hours before check-in. No refund within 48 hours of check-in.

Can I list my land immediately? +

Yes — no approval needed. Create your landowner account, upload photos and details, and go live in minutes. You keep 85% of every booking.

How much does it cost guests? +

Guests pay the listed daily rate + 15% service fee (on rate + cleaning fee) + ~$18 trip protection + Stripe payment processing fee (2.9% + $0.30). No monthly subscription required.
