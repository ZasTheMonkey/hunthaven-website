Florida Outfitters Directory

Licensed guides and outfitters offering fully-guided Florida hunting and fishing experiences. This is a separate category from private land leases.

Important — Outfitters are separate from land listings

Outfitters listed here are not connected to any private land lease listings on LeaseWild. Booking a land lease does not include guide services, and booking an outfitter does not provide private land access through LeaseWild.

What's the difference?

Land Leases vs. Outfitters

These are two completely separate systems on LeaseWild. Here's how they differ:

Private Land Leases

  • ✓ Book exclusive access to private land
  • ✓ Self-guided — hunt/fish on your own terms
  • ✓ Listed and managed by the landowner
  • ✓ Florida only — more states coming
Browse Land Listings

Outfitters (Guide Services)

  • ✓ Fully guided experiences with a licensed guide
  • ✓ Guide provides expertise, gear, and access
  • ✓ Listed and managed by the outfitter
  • ✓ NOT connected to land lease listings
