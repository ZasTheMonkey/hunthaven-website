Before you sign up

It depends on the state and the water. In most states, if a pond is fully enclosed on private land and has no connection to navigable public waterways, a fishing license is not required. LeaseWild verifies this for every fishing listing and shows the specific exemption right on the listing page. No guessing.

Every booking automatically includes $1M in general liability coverage for both the guest and the landowner. A state-compliant digital waiver is signed at checkout. If there's a dispute, our support team mediates.

Payouts go through Stripe directly to your bank account. Landowners keep 85% of each booking. Payouts are issued weekly for all completed trips. No setup fees, no monthly costs.
